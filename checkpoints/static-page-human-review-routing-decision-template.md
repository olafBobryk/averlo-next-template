Status: planned

Role: template

Guide: ../_guides/artifacts/checkpoint.md

## Transition

The static-page strategy template moves from implementation evidence to human
review routing: keep feedback in one page-level packet or split it into
parallel section review packets.

## Applies To

- Shape: `static-page-implementation-strategy-template`
- Workpiece: `static-page-implementation-pass-template`
- Run: none

## Direction

outbound

## Evidence

- Artifact: implementation evidence
- Artifact: human/steward review routing decision

## Decision

The human or steward accepts single page review, accepts parallel section
review, overrides the recommendation, or pauses for more verification.
